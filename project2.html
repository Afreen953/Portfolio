PROJECT 2

BOOSTING AZURE's POWER: A TALE OF OPTIMZATION AND SECURITY

Objective:

Our mission was crystal clear: to fortify Azure infrastructure, making it a bastion of performance and security while aligning with industry best practices.

Tools Used:

PowerShell, JavaScript, Azure CLI, .NET, Microsoft SQL Server, and Azure DevOps

Chapters:

  • Harnessing the Power of Automation:

    With the magical incantations of PowerShell, JavaScript, and Azure CLI, we orchestrated a symphony of automation, ensuring that sensitive data danced to the beats of optimal performance. By adhering to Azure's best practices, we not only improved the efficiency of our Azure network and SQL analytics machines but also gained invaluable insights, paving the way for a smoother, faster journey through the digital landscape.

  • Fortifying the Ramparts:

    Our vigilance never wavered as we delved into the depths of NetSuite, uncovering and vanquishing lurking threats to our on-premise networking and hardware. With a keen eye for detail, we identified and neutralized potential risks and vulnerabilities within the Azure environment, safeguarding our data and standing tall in compliance with best practices.

  • Weaving the Threads of Reliability:

    In the realm of applications, every thread counts. Armed with .NET and Azure DevOps, we meticulously debugged and fortified the Logs web application, ensuring its seamless operation and steadfast performance. Our unwavering commitment to Azure's best practices not only bolstered the application's security but also ushered in an era of enhanced reliability and peace of mind.

  • Optimizing the Heartbeat:

    The beating heart of our system, the Microsoft SQL Server, received our undivided attention. With surgical precision, we optimized its queries, infusing new life into its veins. The result? A system pulsating with increased efficiency and performance, all while upholding the sanctity of data security in alignment with Azure's best practices.
